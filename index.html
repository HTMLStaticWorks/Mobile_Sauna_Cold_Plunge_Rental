The Science of Contrast Therapy

Alternating between hot and cold environments acts like a pump for your cardiovascular system, reducing inflammation and accelerating recovery.

1. Heat (15-20 mins)

Sweat out toxins and relax muscles in our 180°F cedar barrel sauna. Blood vessels dilate, increasing circulation.

2. Cold (1-3 mins)

Plunge into 39°F water. Blood vessels constrict rapidly, flushing out lactic acid and reducing inflammation.

3. Rest (10-15 mins)

Allow your body temperature to normalize. Endorphins are released, creating a profound sense of well-being.

Temperature Protocol Guide

Whether you're just starting out or a seasoned athlete, we have a protocol designed for your comfort and safety.

1

Beginner

  • Sauna: 160°F (10 mins)
  • Plunge: 50°F (30-60 secs)
  • Cycles: 2 Rounds
2

Intermediate

  • Sauna: 180°F (15 mins)
  • Plunge: 42°F (1-2 mins)
  • Cycles: 3-4 Rounds
3

Advanced

  • Sauna: 200°F (20 mins)
  • Plunge: 39°F (3+ mins)
  • Cycles: 4-5 Rounds
