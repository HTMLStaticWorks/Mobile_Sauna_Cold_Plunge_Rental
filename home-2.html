Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to provide for setup?
We need a relatively flat area of at least 10x20 feet (like a driveway or firm lawn). For the cold plunge, we require access to a standard electrical outlet and a water hose spigot to fill the tub.
How is the equipment sanitized?
Hygiene is our top priority. The sauna is deeply cleaned with natural enzymatic cleaners between every rental. The cold plunge water is fully replaced, and the tub is scrubbed and ozone-treated before delivery.
How hot does the sauna get?
Our wood-fired saunas can easily reach 200°F+. However, you have complete control over the temperature by managing the fire size. We recommend 170°F-180°F for optimal contrast therapy.
